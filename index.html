About Al-Gazzali Islamia Kamil Madrasah

Al-Gazzali Islamia Kamil Madrasah (EIN: 102644) was established in 1955 with a vision to provide holistic education. Located at Bhandaria, Pirojpur, Bangladesh, this historic institution has been combining Islamic and modern education for over five decades.

Our mission is to nurture students with knowledge, ethics, and critical thinking. We believe true education is the harmony of character building and intellectual growth.

Abdullah Mahamud
Principal, Al-Gazzali Islamia Kamil Madrasah
"Education is the foundation of a just society. At Al-Gazzali, we cultivate curiosity, respect, and resilience."
